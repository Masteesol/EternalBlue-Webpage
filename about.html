About Eternal Blue

Eternal Blue is a group consisting of me, Marius, and my wife, Francine. We are both students within the IT field. I study frontend web development and Francine studies cyber security.

The artist name comes from the notorious cyberattack exploit called EternalBlue, and we thought it was apt.

The music is written and produced by us and recorded in a small guest room in our apartment, much to our neighbors' dismay.

The music is inspired by a plethora of bands and artists, and lately we’ve taken a liking to synth-wave, which has been an inspiration for the music of the next album we’re working on. That, and the soundtrack of cyberpunk games, like Deus Ex: Human Revolution.
